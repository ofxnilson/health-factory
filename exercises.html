Warm-Up Dude!

Before starting any at-home workout, there's a crucial step you should never skip: Warming up!

Your warm-up doesn’t have to be lengthy—just five minutes to activate your muscles and increase your heart rate. Try easier versions of the exercises you plan to do to prepare your body for the full workout.

Arm circles are an excellent way to loosen up before exercising.

For instance, if your routine includes squats and push-ups, spend a few minutes engaging in light movements to get your blood flowing, like marching in place or doing leg and arm swings.

After that, move on to assisted squats and push-ups against a countertop.

This routine will ensure proper form and reduce the risk of injury.
