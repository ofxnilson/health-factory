The Importance of Exercise

Regular physical activity is essential for maintaining a healthy lifestyle. Exercise improves cardiovascular health, strengthens muscles, enhances flexibility, and boosts mental well-being. Whether you prefer intense workouts or gentle stretching, moving your body daily can lead to a longer, healthier life.
